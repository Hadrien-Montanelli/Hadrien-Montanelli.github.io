About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics Department and the Data Science Institute of the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science at Columbia University. I am working with Qiang Du on the mathematical analysis of Deep Learning models, and on numerical algorithms for nonlocal PDEs with applications in peridynamics.

I received my PhD in Applied Mathematics in 2017 from the University of Oxford where I studied numerical algorithms for differential equations with periodicity with Nick Trefethen.

Prior to this I completed a M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics at the University of Toulouse and a M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering at the French National Higher School of Aeronautics.
