About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scientist in the Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics Department and the Data Science Institute of the Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Sciences of Columbia University.

I received my PhD in Applied Mathematics in 2017 from the University of Oxford where I studied numerical algorithms for differential equations with periodicity with Nick Trefethen.

Prior to this I completed a Masters in Applied Mathematics at the University of Toulouse and a Masters in Aerospace Engineering at the National Higher School of Aeronautics in Toulouse.
