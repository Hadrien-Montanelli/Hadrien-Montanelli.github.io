About

Research interests

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Inria and IP Paris in the IDEFIX team.

My general research interests lie in numerical methods for PDEs and integral equations, inverse problems, approximation theory, and machine learning.

More generally, my background is in numerical analysis and scientific computing.
