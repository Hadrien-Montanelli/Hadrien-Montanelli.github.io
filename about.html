About

I am a Researcher (Chargé de Recherche) in applied mathematics with the IDEFIX team at Inria. I am also a Lecturer (Chargé d'Enseignement) at École Polytechnique.

My general research interests lie in numerical methods for PDEs and integral equations, inverse problems, scientific machine learning, and approximation theory.

Check out my blog, my research, and my publications.
